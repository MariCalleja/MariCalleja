## 👋 Hola! I'm Mari and here you can know a bit about me and see some of my Viz projects.

I’m a Marketing Data Analyst who enjoys turning complex data into clear, actionable insights that help teams make smarter decisions. My work focuses on eCommerce analytics, KPI measurement, A/B testing, and marketing performance, using tools such as SQL, BigQuery, Tableau, GA4, and advanced Excel. I currently support analytics initiatives that inform leadership reviews, budgeting, and cross‑functional planning, and I especially enjoy building dashboards that make data accessible and useful for everyone.

🌱 Background: With studies in Business Administration and a specialization in Marketing, I began my career in accounting for a Costa Rican national company, grew into financial analytics within Accounts Receivable for an international B2B organization, and eventually transitioned into marketing data analytics for a Maine‑based company. This journey has shaped my analytical mindset, business understanding, and appreciation for data‑driven storytelling.

I’m known for being analytical yet approachable, with strong attention to detail, curiosity, and a collaborative mindset. Fluent in English and Spanish, I bring a global perspective, adaptability, and genuine enthusiasm to every project.
